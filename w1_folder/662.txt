<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Material_Safety_Data_Sheet">Material Safety Data Sheet</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Methane_(data_page)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Material Safety Data Sheet">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The handling of this chemical may incur notable safety precautions. It is highly recommend that you seek the Material Safety Datasheet (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Material_safety_data_sheet" title="Material safety data sheet">MSDS</a>) for this chemical from a reliable source such as <a class="external text" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120906072712/http://avogadro.chem.iastate.edu/MSDS/methane.pdf" rel="nofollow">Iowa State</a>, and follow its directions.
</p>
