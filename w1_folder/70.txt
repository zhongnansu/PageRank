<p><i><b>Environmental Research Letters</b></i> is a quarterly, <a href="/wiki/Peer_review" title="Peer review">peer-reviewed</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Open_access_(publishing)" title="Open access (publishing)">open-access</a>, leading international <a href="/wiki/Scientific_journal" title="Scientific journal">scientific journal</a> covering research in all aspects of <a href="/wiki/Environmental_science" title="Environmental science">environmental science</a>. It is produced by <a href="/wiki/IOP_Publishing" title="IOP Publishing">IOP Publishing</a>. <i><b>Environmental Research Letters</b></i> coverage reflects the increasingly interdisciplinary nature of environmental science, recognizing wide-ranging contributions to the development of methods, tools and evaluation strategies relevant to the field. Numerical modelling or simulation, as well as theoretical and experimental approaches to environmental science form the core content. Approaches from a range of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Physical_science" title="Physical science">physical</a> and <a href="/wiki/Natural_science" title="Natural science">natural sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Economics" title="Economics">economics</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Politics" title="Politics">political</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sociology" title="Sociology">sociological</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Legal_studies" title="Legal studies">legal studies</a> are also present.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">editor-in-chief</a> is <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Kammen" title="Daniel Kammen">Daniel Kammen</a> (<a href="/wiki/University_of_California,_Berkeley" title="University of California, Berkeley">University of California, Berkeley</a>).
</p><p>Many of the journal's articles are also covered on IOP Publishing's web site.
</p>
