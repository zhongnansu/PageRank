<p><b>Butterworth–Heinemann</b> is a British <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Publishing_company" title="Publishing company">publishing company</a> specialized in professional information and learning materials for higher education and professional training, in printed and electronic forms. It was formed in 1990 by the merger of <a href="/wiki/Heinemann_(publisher)" title="Heinemann (publisher)">Heinemann</a> Professional Publishing and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Butterworths" title="Butterworths">Butterworths</a> Scientific, both subsidiaries of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Reed_International" title="Reed International">Reed International</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>It has publishing units in <a href="/wiki/Oxford" title="Oxford">Oxford</a> (UK) and <a href="/wiki/Waltham,_Massachusetts" title="Waltham, Massachusetts">Waltham, Massachusetts</a> (United States).
</p><p>As of 2006, it is an <a href="/wiki/Imprint_(trade_name)" title="Imprint (trade name)">imprint</a> of <a href="/wiki/Elsevier" title="Elsevier">Elsevier</a>.
</p>
