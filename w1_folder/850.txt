<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of environmental reports</h1> <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div> <div id="contentSub"></div>
<div id="jump-to-nav"></div> <a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>This is a list of notable <b>environmental reports</b>. In this context they relate to the impacts of human activity on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Environment_(biophysical)" title="Environment (biophysical)">environment</a>.
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/City-level_Decoupling:_Urban_Resource_Flows_and_the_Governance_of_Infrastructure_Transitions" title="City-level Decoupling: Urban Resource Flows and the Governance of Infrastructure Transitions">City-level Decoupling: Urban Resource Flows and the Governance of Infrastructure Transitions</a> – by the <a href="/wiki/International_Resource_Panel" title="International Resource Panel">International Resource Panel</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Clean_Energy_Trends" title="Clean Energy Trends">Clean Energy Trends</a> – a series of reports by Clean Edge – beginning in 2002</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Copeland_Report" title="Copeland Report">Copeland Report</a> – for the U.S. government, completed in 1933</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Copenhagen_Diagnosis" title="Copenhagen Diagnosis">Copenhagen Diagnosis</a> – written by twenty-six climate scientists from eight countries</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dioxin_Reassessment_Report" title="Dioxin Reassessment Report">Dioxin Reassessment Report</a> – by the United States Environmental Protection Agency</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Environmental_Impact_of_the_Big_Cypress_Swamp_Jetport" title="Environmental Impact of the Big Cypress Swamp Jetport">Environmental Impact of the Big Cypress Swamp Jetport</a> ("Leopold Report") – <a href="/wiki/United_States_Department_of_the_Interior" title="United States Department of the Interior">United States Department of the Interior</a> (1969)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Environmental_Risks_and_Challenges_of_Anthropogenic_Metals_Flows_and_Cycles" title="Environmental Risks and Challenges of Anthropogenic Metals Flows and Cycles">Environmental Risks and Challenges of Anthropogenic Metals Flows and Cycles</a> – by the <a href="/wiki/International_Resource_Panel" title="International Resource Panel">International Resource Panel</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Forest_Principles" title="Forest Principles">Forest Principles</a> – United Nations Conference on Environment and Development (UNCED)</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/The_Global_2000_Report_to_the_President" title="The Global 2000 Report to the President">The Global 2000 Report to the President</a></i> – Council on Environmental Quality (1981)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Global_Environment_Outlook" title="Global Environment Outlook">Global Environment Outlook</a> – United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hirsch_report" title="Hirsch report">Hirsch report</a> (<i>Peaking of World Oil Production: Impacts, Mitigation, and Risk Management</i>) – United States Department of Energy</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Index_of_Leading_Environmental_Indicators" title="Index of Leading Environmental Indicators">Index of Leading Environmental Indicators</a></i> – Pacific Research Institute</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/IPCC_First_Assessment_Report" title="IPCC First Assessment Report">IPCC First Assessment Report</a> – Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/IPCC_supplementary_report,_1992" title="IPCC supplementary report, 1992">IPCC supplementary report, 1992</a> – IPCC</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/IPCC_Second_Assessment_Report" title="IPCC Second Assessment Report">IPCC Second Assessment Report</a> – IPCC</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/IPCC_Third_Assessment_Report" title="IPCC Third Assessment Report">IPCC Third Assessment Report</a> <i>Climate Change 2001</i> – IPCC (2001)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/IPCC_Fourth_Assessment_Report" title="IPCC Fourth Assessment Report">IPCC Fourth Assessment Report</a> <i>Climate Change 2007</i> – IPCC (2007)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/IPCC_Fifth_Assessment_Report" title="IPCC Fifth Assessment Report">IPCC Fifth Assessment Report</a> (2014)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Leopold_Report" title="Leopold Report">Leopold Report</a> ("Wildlife Management in the National Parks") – Special Advisory Board on Wildlife Management (1963)</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Livestock%27s_Long_Shadow" title="Livestock's Long Shadow">Livestock's Long Shadow – Environmental Issues and Options</a></i> – United Nations (2006)</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Living_Planet_Report" title="Living Planet Report">Living Planet Report</a></i> – WWF, every two years</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Making_Sweden_an_Oil-Free_Society" title="Making Sweden an Oil-Free Society">Making Sweden an Oil-Free Society</a></i> – Government of Sweden (2006)</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Meat_Atlas" title="Meat Atlas">Meat Atlas</a></i> – published by the <a href="/wiki/Heinrich_B%C3%B6ll_Foundation" title="Heinrich Böll Foundation">Heinrich Böll Foundation</a> and <a href="/wiki/Friends_of_the_Earth_Europe" title="Friends of the Earth Europe">Friends of the Earth Europe</a></li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Nuclear_Power_and_the_Environment" title="Nuclear Power and the Environment">Nuclear Power and the Environment</a></i> – UK <a href="/wiki/Royal_Commission_on_Environmental_Pollution" title="Royal Commission on Environmental Pollution">Royal Commission on Environmental Pollution</a> – 1976</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Our_Common_Future" title="Our Common Future">Our Common Future</a></i> – World Commission on Environment and Development (1987)</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Outlook_On_Renewable_Energy_In_America" title="Outlook On Renewable Energy In America">Outlook On Renewable Energy In America</a></i> (2 volumes) – <a href="/wiki/American_Council_on_Renewable_Energy" title="American Council on Renewable Energy">American Council on Renewable Energy</a> – 2007</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Phase_I_Environmental_Site_Assessment" title="Phase I Environmental Site Assessment">Phase I Environmental Site Assessment</a> – generic</li>
<li><i><a class="external text" href="http://www.thelancet.com/commissions/pollution-and-health/" rel="nofollow">Pollution and Health (Lancet Commission Report on Pollution and Health 2017</a></i></li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/PPS_10" title="PPS 10">Planning Policy Statement 10: Planning for Sustainable Waste Management</a></i> (PPS 10) – British Government</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Renewable_Energy_Sources_and_Climate_Change_Mitigation" title="Renewable Energy Sources and Climate Change Mitigation">Renewable Energy Sources and Climate Change Mitigation</a></i> – <a href="/wiki/United_Nations" title="United Nations">United Nations</a> <a href="/wiki/Intergovernmental_Panel_on_Climate_Change" title="Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change">Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change</a> (IPCC) – planned for 2010</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Report_of_the_Royal_Commission_on_Genetic_Modification" title="Report of the Royal Commission on Genetic Modification">Report of the Royal Commission on Genetic Modification</a></i> – Royal Commission on Genetic Modification (2001)</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/State_of_the_Climate" title="State of the Climate">State of the Climate</a></i> – NOAA/NCDC (published annually)</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/State_of_the_World_(book_series)" title="State of the World (book series)">The State of the World</a></i> – Worldwatch Institute (published yearly since 1984)</li>
<li><i><a class="external text" href="https://cfpub.epa.gov/roe/" rel="nofollow">US Environmental Protection Agency – Report on the Environment</a></i> – US EPA (2016)</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Wegman_Report" title="Wegman Report">Wegman Report</a></i> – to validate criticisms of reconstructions of the <a href="/wiki/Temperature_record_of_the_past_1000_years" title="Temperature record of the past 1000 years">temperature record of the past 1000 years</a></li>
<li><i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Windscale:_Britain%E2%80%99s_Biggest_Nuclear_Disaster" title="Windscale: Britain’s Biggest Nuclear Disaster">Windscale: Britain’s Biggest Nuclear Disaster</a></i> (2007)</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/World_Climate_Report" title="World Climate Report">World Climate Report</a></i> – Greening Earth Society</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/The_World%27s_25_Most_Endangered_Primates" title="The World's 25 Most Endangered Primates">The World's 25 Most Endangered Primates</a></i> – selected and published by the IUCN Species Survival Commission Primate Specialist Group (IUCN/SSC PSG), the International Primatological Society (IPS), and Conservation International (CI)</li></ul>
