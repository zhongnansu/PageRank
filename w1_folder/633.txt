<p><i><b>Current Biology</b></i> is a scientific journal that covers all areas of <a href="/wiki/Biology" title="Biology">biology</a>, especially <a href="/wiki/Molecular_biology" title="Molecular biology">molecular biology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cell_biology" title="Cell biology">cell biology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Genetics" title="Genetics">genetics</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Neurobiology" title="Neurobiology">neurobiology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ecology" title="Ecology">ecology</a> and <a href="/wiki/Evolutionary_biology" title="Evolutionary biology">evolutionary biology</a>. The journal is published twice a month and includes <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Peer-review" title="Peer-review">peer-reviewed</a> research articles, various types of review articles, as well as an editorial magazine section. <i>Current Biology</i> was founded in 1991 by the Current Science group, acquired by <a href="/wiki/Elsevier" title="Elsevier">Elsevier</a> in 1998 and has since 2001 been part of <a href="/wiki/Cell_Press" title="Cell Press">Cell Press</a>, a subdivision of Elsevier.
</p><p>According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">Journal Citation Reports</a></i>, the journal has a 2015 <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">impact factor</a> of 8.983.
</p>
