<p><b>Global and Planetary Change</b> is a monthly <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Peer-reviewed" title="Peer-reviewed">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Scientific_journal" title="Scientific journal">scientific journal</a> covering research into the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Earth_sciences" title="Earth sciences">earth sciences</a>, particularly pertaining to changes in aspects thereof such as sea level and the chemical composition of the atmosphere. It has been published by <a href="/wiki/Elsevier" title="Elsevier">Elsevier</a> since it was established in 1989. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Editors-in-chief" title="Editors-in-chief">editors-in-chief</a> are S.A.P.L. Cloetingh (<a href="/wiki/Utrecht_University" title="Utrecht University">Utrecht University</a>), T.M. Cronin (<a href="/wiki/United_States_Geological_Survey" title="United States Geological Survey">United States Geological Survey</a>), K. McGuffie (<a href="/wiki/University_of_Technology_Sydney" title="University of Technology Sydney">University of Technology Sydney</a>), and H. Oberhänsli (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Museum_f%C3%BCr_Naturkunde" title="Museum für Naturkunde">Museum für Naturkunde</a>). According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">Journal Citation Reports</a></i>, the journal has a 2012 <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">impact factor</a> of 3.155, ranking it 9th out of 45 journals in the category "Geography, Physical".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoS_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-WoS-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
