<p><b>Medicine Bow</b> is a town in <a href="/wiki/Carbon_County,_Wyoming" title="Carbon County, Wyoming">Carbon County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wyoming" title="Wyoming">Wyoming</a>, United States. The population was 284 at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_States_Census,_2010" title="United States Census, 2010">2010 census</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Medicine_Bow,_Wyoming&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Medicine Bow is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Medicine_Bow,_Wyoming&amp;params=41_53_52_N_106_12_10_W_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">41°53′52″N</span> <span class="longitude">106°12′10″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">41.89778°N 106.20278°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">41.89778; -106.20278</span></span></span></a></span> (41.897668, -106.202796).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR1_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR1-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p><p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the town has a total area of 3.46 square miles (8.96 km<sup>2</sup>), all of it land.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Gazetteer_files_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Gazetteer_files-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Medicine_Bow,_Wyoming&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Virginian_Hotel,_Medicine_Bow,_WY.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1498" data-file-width="2613" height="126" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/02/Virginian_Hotel%2C_Medicine_Bow%2C_WY.jpg/220px-Virginian_Hotel%2C_Medicine_Bow%2C_WY.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/02/Virginian_Hotel%2C_Medicine_Bow%2C_WY.jpg/330px-Virginian_Hotel%2C_Medicine_Bow%2C_WY.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/02/Virginian_Hotel%2C_Medicine_Bow%2C_WY.jpg/440px-Virginian_Hotel%2C_Medicine_Bow%2C_WY.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Virginian_Hotel,_Medicine_Bow,_WY.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Virginian Hotel</div></div></div>
<p>A post office called Medicine Bow has been in operation since 1869.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> The community was named after the <a href="/wiki/Medicine_Bow_River" title="Medicine Bow River">Medicine Bow River</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Virginian_Hotel_(Medicine_Bow,_Wyoming)" title="Virginian Hotel (Medicine Bow, Wyoming)">Virginian Hotel</a> is a historic hotel located in Medicine Bow. It was built in 1911 by August Grimm. The hotel was added to the <a href="/wiki/National_Register_of_Historic_Places" title="National Register of Historic Places">National Register of Historic Places</a> in 1978.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nris_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-nris-9">[9]</a></sup>
</p>

<p>As of 2000 the median income for a household in the town was $33,750, and the median income for a family was $35,156. Males had a median income of $41,250 versus $20,536 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the town was $16,420. About 10.3% of families and 11.9% of the population were below the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">poverty line</a>, including 9.2% of those under the age of eighteen and 17.5% of those sixty five or over
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010_census">2010 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Medicine_Bow,_Wyoming&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2010 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FactFinder_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-FactFinder-2">[2]</a></sup> of 2010, there were 284 people, 125 households, and 81 families residing in the town. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 82.1 inhabitants per square mile (31.7/km<sup>2</sup>). There were 182 housing units at an average density of 52.6 per square mile (20.3/km<sup>2</sup>). The racial makeup of the town was 95.1% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 0.4% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(U.S._Census)" title="Race (U.S. Census)">Pacific Islander</a>, 3.9% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(U.S._Census)" title="Race (U.S. Census)">other races</a>, and 0.7% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 6.7% of the population.
</p><p>There were 125 households of which 20.8% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 50.4% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 7.2% had a female householder with no husband present, 7.2% had a male householder with no wife present, and 35.2% were non-families. 29.6% of all households were made up of individuals and 18.4% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.27 and the average family size was 2.79.
</p><p>The median age in the town was 49.3 years. 20.1% of residents were under the age of 18; 3.5% were between the ages of 18 and 24; 21.1% were from 25 to 44; 29.9% were from 45 to 64; and 25.4% were 65 years of age or older. The gender makeup of the town was 53.5% male and 46.5% female.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Economy">Economy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Medicine_Bow,_Wyoming&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Economy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In December 2007, plans were announced for construction of a large coal gasification plant to be built southwest of town.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The plant was expected to start construction in 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-11">[11]</a></sup> In 2014, DKRW Advanced Fuels terminated their contract with Sinopec, the Chinese firm that would have built the plant.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_11-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-11">[11]</a></sup> In 2015, there were plans to downscale the plant,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> and in 2016 the project was put on indefinite hold, partly due to the low cost of crude oil in the US.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Medicine_Bow,_Wyoming&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Public education in the town of Medicine Bow is provided by <a href="/wiki/Carbon_County_School_District_Number_2" title="Carbon County School District Number 2">Carbon County School District #2</a>. Zoned campuses include <a class="external text" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20091117155739/http://www.crb2.k12.wy.us/medicinebow.htm" rel="nofollow">Medicine Bow Elementary School</a> (grades K-6) and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=H.E.M._Junior/Senior_High_School&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="H.E.M. Junior/Senior High School (page does not exist)">H.E.M. Junior/Senior High School</a> <a class="external autonumber" href="http://www.crb2.k12.wy.us/hemshs/" rel="nofollow">[1]</a> (grades 7-12).
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_Popular_Culture">In Popular Culture</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Medicine_Bow,_Wyoming&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: In Popular Culture">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Much of the <a href="/wiki/Western_fiction" title="Western fiction">Western novel</a> <i><a href="/wiki/The_Virginian_(novel)" title="The Virginian (novel)">The Virginian</a></i> and subsequent <a href="/wiki/The_Virginian_(TV_series)" title="The Virginian (TV series)">television series</a> of the same name were set in or near Medicine Bow.
</p>
