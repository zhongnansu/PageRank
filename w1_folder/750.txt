<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>This is an alphabetical list of <a href="/wiki/Environmental_issue" title="Environmental issue">environmental issues</a>, harmful aspects of human activity on the <a href="/wiki/Biophysical_environment" title="Biophysical environment">biophysical environment</a>. They are loosely divided into causes, effects and mitigation, noting that effects are interconnected and can cause new effects.
