<p>The <b>University of Twente</b> (Dutch: <i>Universiteit Twente</i>; <small>Dutch pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Dutch" title="Help:IPA/Dutch">[ynivɛrsiˈtɛit ˈtʋɛntə]</a></span>, <a href="/wiki/Abbreviation" title="Abbreviation">abbr.</a> <b><i lang="nl" title="Dutch language text">UT</i></b>) is a public research <a href="/wiki/University" title="University">university</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Enschede" title="Enschede">Enschede</a>, <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a>. It offers degrees in the fields of <a href="/wiki/Social_science" title="Social science">social sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Exact_sciences" title="Exact sciences">exact sciences</a> and is highly specialized in <a href="/wiki/Engineering" title="Engineering">engineering</a>.
</p><p>The UT collaborates with <a href="/wiki/Delft_University_of_Technology" title="Delft University of Technology">Delft University of Technology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Eindhoven_University_of_Technology" title="Eindhoven University of Technology">Eindhoven University of Technology</a> and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wageningen_University_and_Research_Centre" title="Wageningen University and Research Centre">Wageningen University and Research Centre</a> under the umbrella of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/3TU" title="3TU">4TU</a> and is also a partner in the <a href="/wiki/European_Consortium_of_Innovative_Universities" title="European Consortium of Innovative Universities">European Consortium of Innovative Universities</a> (ECIU). The UT is ranked 65th in the <a href="/wiki/Reuters" title="Reuters">Reuters</a>'s 2017 European Most Innovative Universities, and, 153rd worldwide in 2018 according to the <a href="/wiki/Times_Higher_Education" title="Times Higher Education">Times Higher Education</a> magazine.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=University_of_Twente&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<table class="plainlinks metadata ambox mbox-small-left ambox-content" role="presentation"><tbody><tr><td class="mbox-image"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg"><img alt="[icon]" data-file-height="31" data-file-width="44" height="14" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/20px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/30px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/40px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 2x" width="20"/></a></td><td class="mbox-text"><div class="mbox-text-span">This section <b>needs expansion</b>. <small>You can help by <a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=University_of_Twente&amp;action=edit&amp;section=">adding to it</a>.</small> <small><i>(March 2018)</i></small></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:UTWENTE.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="918" data-file-width="1632" height="124" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/97/UTWENTE.jpg/220px-UTWENTE.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/97/UTWENTE.jpg/330px-UTWENTE.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/97/UTWENTE.jpg/440px-UTWENTE.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:UTWENTE.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>University of Twente entrance</div></div></div>
<p>The university was founded in 1961 as <i>Technische Hogeschool Twente</i>. After Delft and Eindhoven, it was the third higher vocational institute of technology in the Netherlands to become a university. The institution was renamed to Universiteit Twente (University of Twente) in 1986. The Dutch government's decision to locate the university in Enschede, the main city of <a href="/wiki/Twente" title="Twente">Twente</a>, had much to do with the north-eastern province's rich industrial history. Another important consideration was the fact that the local economy needed a boost to compensate for the dwindling textile industry.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Campus">Campus</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=University_of_Twente&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Campus">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Universiteit_Twente_Torentje_van_Drienerlo_2005-07-10.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1200" data-file-width="1600" height="165" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e7/Universiteit_Twente_Torentje_van_Drienerlo_2005-07-10.jpg/220px-Universiteit_Twente_Torentje_van_Drienerlo_2005-07-10.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e7/Universiteit_Twente_Torentje_van_Drienerlo_2005-07-10.jpg/330px-Universiteit_Twente_Torentje_van_Drienerlo_2005-07-10.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e7/Universiteit_Twente_Torentje_van_Drienerlo_2005-07-10.jpg/440px-Universiteit_Twente_Torentje_van_Drienerlo_2005-07-10.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Universiteit_Twente_Torentje_van_Drienerlo_2005-07-10.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Torentje_van_Drienerlo" title="Torentje van Drienerlo">Drienerlo tower</a> on the campus</div></div></div>
<p>The University of Twente was built on the former country estate of Drienerlo, situated between <a href="/wiki/Hengelo" title="Hengelo">Hengelo</a> and Enschede. The 140-hectare estate consists of woodland, meadows and water. Architects Van Tijen and Van Embden designed the first — and so far only — Dutch campus university along <a href="/wiki/Higher_education_in_the_United_States" title="Higher education in the United States">American lines</a> where students and staff live, work and pursue their leisure activities on campus.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Through the years, several other important architects and artists have made their own contributions to the design of the campus.
</p><p>Students and staff at the University of Twente continue to form an active academic community. The Student Union, which is run entirely by students, promotes the interests of all student societies and also manages several premises, including the student social centre in the heart of Enschede.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Besides, over hundred sports, cultural, international, social and business associations are part of the student life in the University of Twente.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Administration">Administration</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=University_of_Twente&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Administration">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The current director of the university is professor Thom Palstra. The other two members of the executive board are Victor van der Chijs (President Executive Board) and Mirjam Built-Spiering (Vice-President)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Faculties">Faculties</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=University_of_Twente&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Faculties">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>There are five Faculties at the University of Twente:
</p>
<ul><li>Behavioural Sciences, Management and Social Sciences (BMS)</li>
<li>Engineering Technology (CTW)</li>
<li>Electrical Engineering, Mathematics and Computer Science (EEMCS)</li>
<li>Science and Technology (TNW)</li>
<li>Faculty of Geo-information Sciences and Earth Observation (formerly known as <a href="/wiki/ITC_Enschede" title="ITC Enschede">ITC Enschede</a>)</li></ul>
<p>Each Faculty is in turn organized into several departments.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=University_of_Twente&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The degree programs at the University of Twente range from <a href="/wiki/Business_administration" title="Business administration">business administration</a> and <a href="/wiki/Psychology" title="Psychology">psychology</a> to <a href="/wiki/Applied_physics" title="Applied physics">applied physics</a>, <a href="/wiki/Engineering" title="Engineering">engineering</a> and <a href="/wiki/Biomedical_technology" title="Biomedical technology">biomedical technology</a>. Most students combine coursework in their major subject with a coherent set of minors in another discipline. A growing number of foreign students are finding their way to the UT. Almost all postgraduate programs (and several undergraduate programs) are taught entirely in English. Half of all PhD students at the UT now come from outside the Netherlands.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (February 2018)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup>
</p><p>In the applied sciences, the emphasis is on <a href="/wiki/Nanotechnology" title="Nanotechnology">nanotechnology</a>, process technology, engineering, <a href="/wiki/Information_and_communications_technology" title="Information and communications technology">information and communication technology</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Biomedical_sciences" title="Biomedical sciences">biomedical sciences</a>.
</p><p>The University of Twente sets great importance on the useful application of knowledge in the society. Patents, lifelong learning programs, and spin-off companies testify to this commitment, as does UT's intensive involvement in research programs that enhance knowledge infrastructure in the Netherlands. So far, the UT has produced over 1,000 <a href="/wiki/University_spin-off" title="University spin-off">spin-off companies</a>; more than any other Dutch university.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> UT is also one of the founders of Novel-T, formerly Stichting <a href="/wiki/Kennispark_Twente" title="Kennispark Twente">Kennispark Twente</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Startup_accelerator" title="Startup accelerator">startup accelerator</a> and support program for university spin-offs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The <a href="/wiki/CWTS_Leiden_Ranking" title="CWTS Leiden Ranking">Leiden Ranking</a> (2016) ranked the University of Twente 127 in Europe.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Webometrics_Ranking_of_World_Universities" title="Webometrics Ranking of World Universities">Webometrics Ranking of World Universities</a> (2016) ranked the University of Twente 215 overall in the world and 75 in Europe.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup>
</p><p>The 2016 <a href="/wiki/QS_World_University_Rankings" title="QS World University Rankings">QS World University Rankings</a> ranked the University of Twente 177 overall in the world.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup>
</p><p>The 2017 <a href="/wiki/Times_Higher_Education_World_University_Rankings" title="Times Higher Education World University Rankings">Times Higher Education World University Rankings</a> ranked the University of Twente 153 overall in the world.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> Also, the University was ranked 82 overall in the world in the field of Engineering and technology and 61 in the field of Computer Science.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2016 <a href="/wiki/Academic_Ranking_of_World_Universities" title="Academic Ranking of World Universities">Academic Ranking of World Universities</a> (ARWU) ranked the University of Twente 301-400 overall in the world.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup>
</p><p>The University of Twente also holds the title of the most entrepreneurial university in Netherlands and was voted the best Dutch university in the area of valorization.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup>
</p>
