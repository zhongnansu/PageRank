<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Carbamino</h1> <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div> <div id="contentSub"></div>
<div id="jump-to-nav"></div> <a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Carbamino</b> refers to a compound composed by the addition of <a href="/wiki/Carbon_dioxide" title="Carbon dioxide">carbon dioxide</a> with a free <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Amino" title="Amino">amino</a> group in an <a href="/wiki/Amino_acid" title="Amino acid">amino acid</a> or a <a href="/wiki/Protein" title="Protein">protein</a>, such as <a href="/wiki/Hemoglobin" title="Hemoglobin">hemoglobin</a> forming <a href="/wiki/Carbaminohemoglobin" title="Carbaminohemoglobin">carbaminohemoglobin</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
