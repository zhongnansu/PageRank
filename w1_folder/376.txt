<p><b>Rapolano Terme</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Siena" title="Province of Siena">Province of Siena</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Tuscany" title="Tuscany">Tuscany</a>, located about 60 kilometres (37 mi) southeast of <a href="/wiki/Florence" title="Florence">Florence</a> and about 20 kilometres (12 mi) east of <a href="/wiki/Siena" title="Siena">Siena</a> in the area known as the <a href="/wiki/Crete_Senesi" title="Crete Senesi">Crete Senesi</a>.
</p><p>Until 1949 it was known simply as <b>Rapolano</b>.
</p><p>The municipality includes several minor towns, among which the village of <a href="/wiki/Serre_di_Rapolano" title="Serre di Rapolano">Serre di Rapolano</a>.
</p>
