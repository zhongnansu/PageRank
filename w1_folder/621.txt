<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Marsh gas</h1> <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div> <div id="contentSub"></div>
<div id="jump-to-nav"></div> <a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Marsh gas, swamp gas</b> and  <b>bog gas</b> is a mixture of <a href="/wiki/Methane" title="Methane">methane</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hydrogen_sulfide" title="Hydrogen sulfide">hydrogen sulfide </a> and <a href="/wiki/Carbon_dioxide" title="Carbon dioxide">carbon dioxide</a>, produced naturally within some geographical <a href="/wiki/Marsh" title="Marsh">marshes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Swamp" title="Swamp">swamps</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Bog" title="Bog">bogs</a>.  
</p><p>The surface of marshes, swamps and bogs is initially <a href="/wiki/Porous_medium" title="Porous medium">porous</a> vegetation that <a href="/wiki/Decomposition" title="Decomposition">rots</a> to form a <a href="/wiki/Soil_crust" title="Soil crust">crust</a> that prevents <a href="/wiki/Oxygen" title="Oxygen">oxygen</a> from reaching the organic material  trapped below. That is the condition that allows <a href="/wiki/Anaerobic_digestion" title="Anaerobic digestion">anaerobic digestion</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fermentation" title="Fermentation">fermentation</a> of any <a href="/wiki/Plant" title="Plant">plant</a> or <a href="/wiki/Animal" title="Animal">animal</a> material which incidentally also produces methane.
</p><p>In some cases there is sufficient <a href="/wiki/Heat" title="Heat">heat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fuel" title="Fuel">fuel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Oxygen" title="Oxygen">oxygen</a> to allow <a href="/wiki/Spontaneous_combustion" title="Spontaneous combustion">spontaneous combustion</a> and underground fires to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Smolder" title="Smolder">smolder</a> for some considerable time as occurred at a natural reserve in Spain.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Such fires can cause surface <a href="/wiki/Subsidence" title="Subsidence">subsidence</a> presenting an unpredictable physical hazard and as well as environmental changes or damage to the local environment and the <a href="/wiki/Ecosystem" title="Ecosystem">ecosystem</a> it supports.
</p>
