<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_business_and_management">In business and management</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Process&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: In business and management">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Business_process" title="Business process">Business process</a>, activities that produce a specific service or product for customers</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Business_process_modeling" title="Business process modeling">Business process modeling</a>, activity of representing processes of an enterprise in order to deliver improvements</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Manufacturing_process_management" title="Manufacturing process management">Manufacturing process management</a>, a collection of technologies and methods used to define how products are to be manufactured.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Process_architecture" title="Process architecture">Process architecture</a>, structural design of processes, applies to fields such as computers, business processes, logistics, project management</li>
<li>Process costing, a cost allocation procedure of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Managerial_accounting" title="Managerial accounting">managerial accounting</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Process_management" title="Process management">Process management</a>, ensemble of activities of planning and monitoring the performance of a business process or manufacturing processes</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Process_management_(Project_Management)" title="Process management (Project Management)">Process management (Project Management)</a> , a systematic series of activities directed towards causing an end result in engineering activities or project management</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Process-based_management" title="Process-based management">Process-based management</a>, is a management approach that views a business as a collection of processes</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Process_industry" title="Process industry">Process industry</a>, a category of material-related industry</li></ul>

<li><a href="/wiki/Service_of_process" title="Service of process">Service of process</a>, the procedure of giving official notice of a legal proceeding</li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="In_mathematics">In mathematics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Process&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: In mathematics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul><li>In <a href="/wiki/Probability_theory" title="Probability theory">probability theory</a>:
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Branching_process" title="Branching process">Branching process</a>, a Markov process that models a population</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/Predictable_process" title="Predictable process">Predictable process</a>, a stochastic process whose value is knowable</li>
