<p><i><b>Nature Geoscience</b></i> is a monthly <a href="/wiki/Peer_review" title="Peer review">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Scientific_journal" title="Scientific journal">scientific journal</a> published by the <a href="/wiki/Nature_Publishing_Group" title="Nature Publishing Group">Nature Publishing Group</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">editor-in-chief</a> is Heike Langenberg. It was established in January 2008.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Scope">Scope</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nature_Geoscience&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Scope">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The journal covers all aspects of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Earth_sciences" title="Earth sciences">Earth sciences</a>, including <a href="/wiki/Theory" title="Theory">theoretical research</a>, <a href="/wiki/Conceptual_model" title="Conceptual model">modelling</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Field_work" title="Field work">field work</a>. Significant related work in other fields, such as  <a href="/wiki/Atmospheric_sciences" title="Atmospheric sciences">atmospheric sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Geology" title="Geology">geology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Geophysics" title="Geophysics">geophysics</a>, <a href="/wiki/Climatology" title="Climatology">climatology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oceanography" title="Oceanography">oceanography</a>, <a href="/wiki/Paleontology" title="Paleontology">palaeontology</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Space_science" title="Space science">space science</a>, is also published. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-about_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-about-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
