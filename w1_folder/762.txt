<p><b>Radical Routes</b> is a <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>-based <a href="/wiki/Cooperative_federation" title="Cooperative federation">cooperative federation</a>. The organisation provides <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mutual_aid_(organization)" title="Mutual aid (organization)">mutual aid</a> and lends money to <a href="/wiki/Housing_cooperative" title="Housing cooperative">housing</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Worker_co-operative" title="Worker co-operative">worker co-operatives</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UK_Social_Centre_Network" title="UK Social Centre Network">social centres</a>. Members of the network share information about social change.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Work">Work</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Radical_Routes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Work">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Radical Routes provides small loans (up to about £50,000) to member co-operatives. Decisions on making loans are made collectively by other member co-operatives (this is described as "peer-group loan appraisal"). 
</p><p>Radical Routes publishes booklets on subjects such as how to set up a housing co-operative, how to set up a worker co-operative and how to work out your <a href="/wiki/Ecological_footprint" title="Ecological footprint">ecological footprint</a>. The publication are available free of charge, either as a download or in print.
</p><p>With <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cooperatives_UK" title="Cooperatives UK">Cooperatives UK</a> they launched proposals for an independent cooperative regulator after the government announced that it would be abolishing the <a href="/wiki/Financial_Services_Authority" title="Financial Services Authority">FSA</a>. Previously the FSA had regulated co-operatives, which were registered as <a href="/wiki/Industrial_and_provident_society" title="Industrial and provident society">industrial and provident societies</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Members">Members</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Radical_Routes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Members">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Member co-operatives are expected to commit to a share of Radical Routes' workload as volunteers, and work toward radical social change.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pn_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-pn-1">[1]</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Affiliations">Affiliations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Radical_Routes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Affiliations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
