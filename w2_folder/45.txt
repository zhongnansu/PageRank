<p>A <b>Green Building Council (GBC)</b> is national non-profit, non-government organization that is part of a global network recognized by the <a href="/wiki/World_Green_Building_Council" title="World Green Building Council">World Green Building Council</a>. A GBC's goal is promote a transformation of the <a href="/wiki/Built_environment" title="Built environment">built environment</a> towards one that is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sustainable" title="Sustainable">sustainable</a> (buildings and cities that are environmentally sensitive, economically viable, socially just and culturally significant).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="List_of_recognised_GBCs">List of recognised GBCs</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Green_Building_Council&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: List of recognised GBCs">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As of October 2012, there were at least 20 nations with established GBCs, seven recognized as "emerging" members, and dozens more in the development process.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> As of Spring 2014 there were around 100 Green Building Councils at various stages of their development.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2018)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup>
</p><p>The Green Building Council Russia (RuGBC) formed in 2009 and is presently seeking Emerging Status.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2018)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> Growth in the <a href="/wiki/Commonwealth_of_Independent_States" title="Commonwealth of Independent States">CIS</a> countries accompanies growth in the number of Green Construction projects in those countries, that is, those certified to <a href="/wiki/Leadership_in_Energy_and_Environmental_Design" title="Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design">LEED</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2018)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup>
