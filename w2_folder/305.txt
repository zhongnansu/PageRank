<p>The <b>Asia Pacific Greens Federation</b> is a federation of national Green parties, social and environmental organizations in countries in the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">Pacific Ocean</a> and <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a>, and is one of the four federations that constitute the <a href="/wiki/Global_Greens" title="Global Greens">Global Greens</a>. 32 Parties from 30 nations got together in February 2005 in <a href="/wiki/Kyoto" title="Kyoto">Kyoto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>, to found the network. There they elected a Membership Panel, and delegates to the Global Greens Coordination (GGC).
</p><p>Thereafter newly endorsed Member parties and groups participated at the Global Greens Conference in São Paulo, Brazil in 2008, and in the 2nd APGN Congress held in Taipei City (Taiwan) in April 2010. This Congress adopted a new organisational structure for the APGN, known as the (<a class="external text" href="http://apgn2010.org/rules-apgn" rel="nofollow">APGN rules</a><sup class="noprint Inline-Template"><span style="white-space: nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Link_rot" title="Wikipedia:Link rot"><span title=" Dead link since October 2016">permanent dead link</span></a></i>]</span></sup>, and a <a class="external text" href="http://apgn2010.org/apgn-5-year-strategic-plan" rel="nofollow">5 Year Strategic Plan (2011-2015)</a><sup class="noprint Inline-Template"><span style="white-space: nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Link_rot" title="Wikipedia:Link rot"><span title=" Dead link since October 2016">permanent dead link</span></a></i>]</span></sup>. A new APGN Coordinating Committee (APGCC) was elected, composed of 8 delegates from different countries (Australia, New Zealand, India, Taiwan, Korea, Philippines, Japan, Sri Lanka). Also the 3 new delegates of GGC from Asia Pacific were elected. Other <a class="external text" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20101026043949/http://www.apgn2010.org/apgn-congress-outcomes" rel="nofollow">outcomes</a> included resolutions and a Fair Share Declaration. The <a class="external text" href="http://www.apgn2010.org/apgn-congress-final-report" rel="nofollow">final report</a><sup class="noprint Inline-Template"><span style="white-space: nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Link_rot" title="Wikipedia:Link rot"><span title=" Dead link since October 2016">permanent dead link</span></a></i>]</span></sup> details the proceedings and outcomes of the 2010 Congress in Taipei.
</p>
