<p><i><b>Greensburg</b></i> is an American <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Television_series" title="Television series">television series</a> broadcast on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Planet_Green" title="Planet Green">Planet Green</a> television network. The show takes place in <a href="/wiki/Greensburg,_Kansas" title="Greensburg, Kansas">Greensburg, Kansas</a>, and is about rebuilding the town in a <a href="/wiki/Sustainability" title="Sustainability">sustainable</a> way after being hit by the May 2007 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Greensburg_tornado" title="Greensburg tornado">EF5 tornado</a>.
</p>
