<p><b>The Greenhouse</b> (in Hebrew: "החממה";  Ha-Hamama) is a television series by Giora Chamizer. The series was created and aired in <a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a> in 2012 and was sold to <a href="/wiki/Nickelodeon_(UK_and_Ireland)" title="Nickelodeon (UK and Ireland)">Nickelodeon UK</a> in 2013.
The series is about a school for gifted in the sea of Galilee in Israel. The school has two clubs: The Eagles and The Ravens, with the Ravens as the smarter house and the Eagles as the more athletic house. The students learn how to be leaders that will save the world.
</p><p>An American adaptation under the title of <a href="/wiki/Greenhouse_Academy" title="Greenhouse Academy">Greenhouse Academy</a> premiered on <a href="/wiki/Netflix" title="Netflix">Netflix</a> in September 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
