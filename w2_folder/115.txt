<p><b>Green Mansion</b> is a historic home and later commercial building located at <a href="/wiki/Newark,_Delaware" title="Newark, Delaware">Newark</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_Castle_County,_Delaware" title="New Castle County, Delaware">New Castle County, Delaware</a>.  It was built about 1882 and is an oblong, three-story, brick building with a flat roofline. It features a green <a href="/wiki/Serpentinite" title="Serpentinite">serpentine</a> facade with a two-story bay window at each end, joined in the center by an ornate two-tiered porch featuring paneled columns.  It was originally built as a two-story residence and expanded in the early 20th century when it began being used for commercial purposes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nom_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-nom-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>It was added to the <a href="/wiki/National_Register_of_Historic_Places" title="National Register of Historic Places">National Register of Historic Places</a> in 1983.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nris_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-nris-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
