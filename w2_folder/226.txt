<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Evergreen, Florida</h1> <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div> <div id="contentSub"></div>
<div id="jump-to-nav"></div> <a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Evergreen</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> in <a href="/wiki/Nassau_County,_Florida" title="Nassau County, Florida">Nassau County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Florida" title="Florida">Florida</a>, United States. It is located on <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=County_Road_108,_Nassau_County,_Florida&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="County Road 108, Nassau County, Florida (page does not exist)">County Road 108</a>, near the center of the county.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Florida&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Evergreen is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Evergreen,_Florida&amp;params=30.7_N_81.7522_W_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">30°42′00″N</span> <span class="longitude">81°45′08″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">30.7°N 81.7522°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">30.7; -81.7522</span></span></span></a></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span id="coordinates"><a href="/wiki/Geographic_coordinate_system" title="Geographic coordinate system">Coordinates</a>: <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Evergreen,_Florida&amp;params=30.7_N_81.7522_W_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">30°42′00″N</span> <span class="longitude">81°45′08″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">30.7°N 81.7522°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">30.7; -81.7522</span></span></span></a></span></span></span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR1-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Area_roads">Area roads</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Florida&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Area roads">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Evergreen lies along a large curve in County Road 108, which is the only paved road in the area.  Sand Hill Road intersects from the south and travels south.  There are several driveways which also have road names appearing on a map of the area.
</p>
