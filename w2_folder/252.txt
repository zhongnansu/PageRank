<p><i><b>Evergreens</b></i> is a compilation album by Italian singer <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mina_(singer)" title="Mina (singer)">Mina</a>, issued in 1974 and distributed only on audio cassette.
</p><p>All the songs were previously published on other albums, except for "Lamento d'amore", released in 1973 on a 45rpm.
</p>
