<p>The <b>Canadian Green Building Awards</b> are a joint program between the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canadian_Green_Building_Council" title="Canadian Green Building Council">Canadian Green Building Council</a> and Sustainable Architecture &amp; Building Magazine (SABMag). The CaGB Awards are awarded annually to both residential and non-residential projects across <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a> of excellent and innovative design and execution. Eligible projects range from new <a href="/wiki/Construction" title="Construction">construction</a>, existing buildings, <a href="/wiki/Interior_design" title="Interior design">interior design</a> projects, and <a href="/wiki/Renovation" title="Renovation">renovations</a> and <a href="/wiki/Retrofitting" title="Retrofitting">retrofits</a>. Entry submission kits are available in January of every year, and the judging occurs in March. Winning projects are announced in May, and publicized in the summer issue of SABMag, on the SABMag and the CaGBC website, and at the CaGBC National Conference.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Jury">Jury</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Canadian_Green_Building_Awards&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Jury">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Canadian Green Building Awards typically has a jury of 4 people who work in the <a href="/wiki/Architecture" title="Architecture">architecture</a> industry, specializing in <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_architecture" title="Sustainable architecture">sustainability</a> and <a href="/wiki/Efficient_energy_use" title="Efficient energy use">energy efficiency</a>. These jury members change annually.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notable_Projects">Notable Projects</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Canadian_Green_Building_Awards&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Notable Projects">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Canadian Green Building Awards chooses notable projects of all different uses, from all around Canada.
</p>
