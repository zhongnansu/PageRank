<p>The <b>August G. and Theresa Green House</b> is a historic house located at 1501 Main Street in <a href="/wiki/Stevens_Point,_Wisconsin" title="Stevens Point, Wisconsin">Stevens Point, Wisconsin</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description_and_history">Description and history</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=August_G._and_Theresa_Green_House&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description and history">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The presence of the <a href="/wiki/Queen_Anne_style_architecture" title="Queen Anne style architecture">Queen Anne style</a> of architecture in the house is easily identifiable by the picturesque silhouette created by its steeply pitched roof of irregular shape and tall chimneys. It was added to the <a href="/wiki/National_Register_of_Historic_Places" title="National Register of Historic Places">National Register of Historic Places</a> on June 1, 2005.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nris_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-nris-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
