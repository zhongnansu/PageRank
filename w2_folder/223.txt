<p><b>Evergreen</b> is a neighborhood and district of <a href="/wiki/San_Jose,_California" title="San Jose, California">San Jose, California</a>, in <a href="/wiki/East_San_Jose" title="East San Jose">Eastside San Jose</a>. Evergreen is bounded by Tully Road to the north, Capitol Expressway to the west, San Felipe Road to the south and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/East_Foothills,_California" title="East Foothills, California">East Foothills</a> neighborhood on the eastern edge.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_San_Jose&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Evergreen_Branch_of_the_San_Jose_Public_Library.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="385" data-file-width="1000" height="85" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/87/Evergreen_Branch_of_the_San_Jose_Public_Library.jpg/220px-Evergreen_Branch_of_the_San_Jose_Public_Library.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/87/Evergreen_Branch_of_the_San_Jose_Public_Library.jpg/330px-Evergreen_Branch_of_the_San_Jose_Public_Library.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/87/Evergreen_Branch_of_the_San_Jose_Public_Library.jpg/440px-Evergreen_Branch_of_the_San_Jose_Public_Library.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Evergreen_Branch_of_the_San_Jose_Public_Library.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The Evergreen branch of the <a href="/wiki/San_Jos%C3%A9_Public_Library" title="San José Public Library">San José Public Library</a>.</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Lake_Cunningham_1.1.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="2016" data-file-width="3584" height="124" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/Lake_Cunningham_1.1.jpg/220px-Lake_Cunningham_1.1.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/Lake_Cunningham_1.1.jpg/330px-Lake_Cunningham_1.1.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/Lake_Cunningham_1.1.jpg/440px-Lake_Cunningham_1.1.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Lake_Cunningham_1.1.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lake_Cunningham" title="Lake Cunningham">Lake Cunningham</a>.</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:302px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Panoramic_of_Evergreen.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="3645" data-file-width="11054" height="99" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/47/Panoramic_of_Evergreen.jpg/300px-Panoramic_of_Evergreen.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/47/Panoramic_of_Evergreen.jpg/450px-Panoramic_of_Evergreen.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/47/Panoramic_of_Evergreen.jpg/600px-Panoramic_of_Evergreen.jpg 2x" width="300"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Panoramic_of_Evergreen.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Panorama of Evergreen.</div></div></div>
<p>Evergreen was the site of gliding experiments in 1911 by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/John_J._Montgomery" title="John J. Montgomery">John J. Montgomery</a>, an important figure in pioneering aeronautics. In his honor the local community has established John J. Montgomery Elementary School, Montgomery Park (City of San Jose)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>.
</p><p>Montgomery's last glider, named "The Evergreen", after the San Jose district, was restored by the <a href="/wiki/Smithsonian_Institution" title="Smithsonian Institution">Smithsonian Institution</a> and is currently on display at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/San_Diego_Air_and_Space_Museum" title="San Diego Air and Space Museum">San Diego Air and Space Museum</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/San_Diego,_California" title="San Diego, California">San Diego, California</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_San_Jose&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Evergreen is located on the far eastern edge of <a href="/wiki/Santa_Clara_Valley" title="Santa Clara Valley">Santa Clara Valley</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Diablo_Range" title="Diablo Range">Diablo Range</a> bordering the area to the east.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_San_Jose&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The area is served by <a href="/wiki/Evergreen_Elementary_School_District" title="Evergreen Elementary School District">Evergreen Elementary School District</a> and <a href="/wiki/East_Side_Union_High_School_District" title="East Side Union High School District">East Side Union High School District</a>. <a href="/wiki/Evergreen_Valley_High_School" title="Evergreen Valley High School">Evergreen Valley High School</a> and <a href="/wiki/Silver_Creek_High_School_(San_Jose,_California)" title="Silver Creek High School (San Jose, California)">Silver Creek High School</a> are the primary comprehensive high schools to serve Evergreen.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Evergreen_Valley_College" title="Evergreen Valley College">Evergreen Valley College</a> is located near the eastern edge of Evergreen.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>Evergreen area is served by two libraries as part of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/San_Jose_Public_Library_System" title="San Jose Public Library System">San Jose Public Library System</a>: the Evergreen Branch Library<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> on Aborn Road and Village Square Branch Library<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> at the Village Square.
</p>
