<p><i><b>Evergreen</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> album by <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> banjoist <a href="/wiki/Alison_Brown" title="Alison Brown">Alison Brown</a>, released in 2008. 
</p><p>Arranged in a jazzy style, the album features fiddler/mandolinists <a href="/wiki/Joe_Craven" title="Joe Craven">Joe Craven</a> and is often compared with similar <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christmas_album" title="Christmas album">Christmas album</a> effort by <a href="/wiki/B%C3%A9la_Fleck_and_the_Flecktones" title="Béla Fleck and the Flecktones">Béla Fleck and the Flecktones</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/Jingle_All_the_Way_(B%C3%A9la_Fleck_and_the_Flecktones_album)" title="Jingle All the Way (Béla Fleck and the Flecktones album)">Jingle All the Way</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AM_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-AM-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
