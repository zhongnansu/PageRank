<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of Principals of Green Templeton College, Oxford</h1> <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div> <div id="contentSub"></div>
<div id="jump-to-nav"></div> <a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>A <b>list of Principals of Green Templeton College, Oxford</b>. The head of <a href="/wiki/Green_Templeton_College,_Oxford" title="Green Templeton College, Oxford">Green Templeton College</a>, <a href="/wiki/University_of_Oxford" title="University of Oxford">University of Oxford</a> is called the <a href="/wiki/Master_(college)" title="Master (college)">Principal</a>. This article also includes the heads of the predecessors institutions of the college, Green College and Templeton College. The current Principal is <a href="/wiki/Denise_Lievesley" title="Denise Lievesley">Denise Lievesley</a>, since October 2015.
</p>
