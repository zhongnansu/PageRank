<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>This is a <b>list of countries by total <a href="/wiki/Greenhouse_gas" title="Greenhouse gas">greenhouse gas</a> (GHG) emissions</b> in 2013. It is based on data for <a href="/wiki/Carbon_dioxide" title="Carbon dioxide">carbon dioxide</a>, <a href="/wiki/Methane" title="Methane">methane</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nitrous_oxide" title="Nitrous oxide">nitrous oxide</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Perfluorocarbon" title="Perfluorocarbon">perfluorocarbon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hydrofluorocarbon" title="Hydrofluorocarbon">hydrofluorocarbon</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sulfur_hexafluoride" title="Sulfur hexafluoride">sulfur hexafluoride</a> emissions compiled by the <a href="/wiki/World_Resources_Institute" title="World Resources Institute">World Resources Institute</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The emissions data shown below do not include <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Land_use,_land-use_change_and_forestry" title="Land use, land-use change and forestry">land-use change and forestry</a>.
</p>
