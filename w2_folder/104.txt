<p><b>Isaiah Greenhouse</b> (born July 15, 1987) is a former <a href="/wiki/American_football" title="American football">American football</a> <a href="/wiki/Linebacker" title="Linebacker">linebacker</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fullback_(American_football)" title="Fullback (American football)">fullback</a>. He was signed by the <a href="/wiki/Houston_Texans" title="Houston Texans">Houston Texans</a> as an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Undrafted_free_agent" title="Undrafted free agent">undrafted free agent</a> in <a href="/wiki/2010_NFL_season" title="2010 NFL season">2010</a>. He played college football at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Northwestern_State_Demons" title="Northwestern State Demons">Northwestern State</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Professional_career">Professional career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isaiah_Greenhouse&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Professional career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Houston_Texans">Houston Texans</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isaiah_Greenhouse&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Houston Texans">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After going <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Undrafted_sportsperson" title="Undrafted sportsperson">undrafted</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2010_NFL_Draft" title="2010 NFL Draft">2010 NFL Draft</a>, Greenhouse was signed by the <a href="/wiki/Houston_Texans" title="Houston Texans">Houston Texans</a> on May 18, 2010. They tried him at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fullback_(American_football)" title="Fullback (American football)">fullback</a>, but moved him back to linebacker later. He was cut on September 3, 2010, and signed to the <a href="/wiki/Practice_squad" title="Practice squad">practice squad</a> two days later. He was called up from the practice squad on October 3, 2010, but was waived on October 10, after being on the roster for one week. He was re-signed to the practice squad on November 3, 2010. Greenhouse was promoted on December 29, 2010. He was re-signed on January 5, 2011. The Texans released him on February 18, 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Dallas_Cowboys">Dallas Cowboys</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isaiah_Greenhouse&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Dallas Cowboys">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Greenhouse was signed by the <a href="/wiki/Dallas_Cowboys" title="Dallas Cowboys">Dallas Cowboys</a> on March 1, 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>On August 16, 2011, he was moved to fullback. He was waived on September 3, 2011, and was signed to the <a href="/wiki/Practice_squad" title="Practice squad">practice squad</a> the next day. He was waived/injured on August 23, 2012.
</p>
