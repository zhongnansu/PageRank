<p><b>Evergreen</b> is a former settlement in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles_County,_California" title="Los Angeles County, California">Los Angeles County</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gnis_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-gnis-1">[1]</a></sup> It lay at an elevation of 190 feet (58 m).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gnis_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-gnis-1">[1]</a></sup> Evergreen still appeared on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/USGS" title="USGS">USGS</a> maps as of 1925.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gnis_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-gnis-1">[1]</a></sup>  Today, the area is part of <a href="/wiki/Whittier,_California" title="Whittier, California">Whittier, California</a>, near the Whittier Quad and the Five Points area.  A school in Whittier still bears the name Evergreen.
</p>
