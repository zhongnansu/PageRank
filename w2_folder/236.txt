<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">For the Evergreen in western Columbus County, see <a href="/wiki/Evergreen,_Tatums_Township,_Columbus_County,_North_Carolina" title="Evergreen, Tatums Township, Columbus County, North Carolina">Evergreen, Tatums Township, Columbus County, North Carolina</a>.</div>
<p><span style="font-size: small;"><span id="coordinates"><a href="/wiki/Geographic_coordinate_system" title="Geographic coordinate system">Coordinates</a>: <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Evergreen,_Ransom_Township,_Columbus_County,_North_Carolina&amp;params=34_17_46_N_78_13_04_W_"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">34°17′46″N</span> <span class="longitude">78°13′04″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">34.29611°N 78.21778°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">34.29611; -78.21778</span></span></span></a></span></span></span>
<b>Evergreen</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ransom_Township,_Columbus_County,_North_Carolina&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ransom Township, Columbus County, North Carolina (page does not exist)">Ransom Township</a>, <a href="/wiki/Columbus_County,_North_Carolina" title="Columbus County, North Carolina">Columbus County</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina" title="North Carolina">North Carolina</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>, located about 1 mile south of <a href="/wiki/Delco,_North_Carolina" title="Delco, North Carolina">Delco</a>.  It lies at an elevation of 39 feet (12 m).
</p>
