<p><b>Evergreen</b> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Laclede_County,_Missouri" title="Laclede County, Missouri">Laclede County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Missouri" title="Missouri">Missouri</a>, United States. The population was 28 at the 2010 census.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census_2010_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census_2010-4">[4]</a></sup> It was named <b>Twin Bridges</b> at the 2000 census.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Missouri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Evergreen is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Evergreen,_Missouri&amp;params=37_32_12_N_92_35_5_W_region:US_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">37°32′12″N</span> <span class="longitude">92°35′5″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">37.53667°N 92.58472°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">37.53667; -92.58472</span></span></span></a></span> (37.536598, -92.584594).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR1_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR1-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p><p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the village has a total area of 10.21 square miles (26.44 km<sup>2</sup>), of which, 10.11 square miles (26.18 km<sup>2</sup>) of it is land and 0.10 square miles (0.26 km<sup>2</sup>) is water.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Gazetteer_files_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Gazetteer_files-1">[1]</a></sup>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010_census">2010 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Missouri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2010 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FactFinder_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-FactFinder-2">[2]</a></sup> of 2010, there were 28 people, 10 households, and 8 families residing in the village. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 2.8 inhabitants per square mile (1.1/km<sup>2</sup>). There were 13 housing units at an average density of 1.3 per square mile (0.5/km<sup>2</sup>). The racial makeup of the village was 92.9% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 3.6% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, and 3.6% from two or more races.
</p><p>There were 10 households of which 40.0% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 70.0% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 10.0% had a female householder with no husband present, and 20.0% were non-families. 0.0% of all households were made up of individuals. The average household size was 2.80 and the average family size was 2.75.
</p><p>The median age in the village was 38.5 years. 21.4% of residents were under the age of 18; 3.6% were between the ages of 18 and 24; 28.6% were from 25 to 44; 42.8% were from 45 to 64; and 3.6% were 65 years of age or older. The gender makeup of the village was 64.3% male and 35.7% female.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2000_census">2000 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Missouri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2000 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR2-8">[8]</a></sup> of 2000, there were 42 people, 13 households, and 10 families residing in the village. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 4.9 people per square mile (1.9/km²). There were 16 housing units at an average density of 1.9 per square mile (0.7/km²). The racial makeup of the village was 95.24% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a> and 4.76% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 4.76% of the population.
</p><p>There were 13 households out of which 69.2% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 69.2% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 7.7% had a female householder with no husband present, and 15.4% were non-families. 7.7% of all households were made up of individuals and 7.7% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 3.23 and the average family size was 3.45.
</p><p>In the village, the population was spread out with 38.1% under the age of 18, 9.5% from 18 to 24, 40.5% from 25 to 44, 9.5% from 45 to 64, and 2.4% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 26 years. For every 100 females, there were 121.1 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 100.0 males.
</p><p>The median income for a household in the village was $31,875, and the median income for a family was $30,625. Males had a median income of $29,583 versus $13,750 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the village was $11,925. None of the population and none of the families were below the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">poverty line</a>.
</p>
