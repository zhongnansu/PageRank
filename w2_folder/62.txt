<p><b>Greenhouse</b> was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bar_(establishment)" title="Bar (establishment)">bar</a>/<a href="/wiki/Restaurant" title="Restaurant">restaurant</a> at <a href="/wiki/100_St_Georges_Terrace" title="100 St Georges Terrace">100 St Georges Terrace</a> in <a href="/wiki/Perth" title="Perth">Perth</a>, Western Australia. Designed by Dutch-born florist, artist, builder and environmentalist Joost Bakker, and opened in 2009,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-design100_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-design100-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-architectureau_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-architectureau-2">[2]</a></sup> it is a <i>"quirky, eco-friendly restaurant"</i> concept,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-smh_13-11-2010_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-smh_13-11-2010-3">[3]</a></sup> which has been described as <i>"... a breath of fresh air and a brilliant example of innovation in the restaurant sector."</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wa_21-09-2010_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-wa_21-09-2010-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p>As a concept, Greenhouse had a mission to improve vastly on the ways restaurants are created, to have better design, better operation, and to be "completely waste free from the ground up".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-design100_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-design100-1">[1]</a></sup>  Amongst other things, Greenhouse <i>"... has its vegetable garden on the roof, grinds its own organic flour, has walls made of hay bales and boasts a zero-carbon footprint."</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wa_21-09-2010_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-wa_21-09-2010-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p>The Greenhouse concept has also appeared, in temporary, "pop-up" restaurant form, at <a href="/wiki/Federation_Square" title="Federation Square">Federation Square</a> in <a href="/wiki/Melbourne" title="Melbourne">Melbourne</a> in 2008-09,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-design100_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-design100-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-architectureau_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-architectureau-2">[2]</a></sup> at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sydney_Harbour" title="Sydney Harbour">Sydney Harbour</a> in 2011,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-design100_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-design100-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wa_25-03-2011_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-wa_25-03-2011-5">[5]</a></sup> and at the <a href="/wiki/Melbourne_Food_and_Wine_Festival" title="Melbourne Food and Wine Festival">Melbourne Food and Wine Festival</a> in 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-design100_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-design100-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-architectureau_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-architectureau-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wa_11-03-2012_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-wa_11-03-2012-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p><p>The head chef at Greenhouse was Matt Stone.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wa_25-03-2011_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-wa_25-03-2011-5">[5]</a></sup>  Both he and the restaurant have won a number of awards.  In 2010, Stone was named Best New Talent at the national Gourmet Traveller Awards;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wa_24-08-2010_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-wa_24-08-2010-7">[7]</a></sup> then was awarded Young Chef of the Year by <i><a href="/wiki/The_West_Australian" title="The West Australian">The West Australian</a> Good Food Guide</i> in 2011<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wa_21-09-2010_4-2"><a href="#cite_note-wa_21-09-2010-4">[4]</a></sup> and 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-miettas_review_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-miettas_review-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p><p>The restaurant was given a one star rating, and the award for Best New Restaurant of the Year, by <i><a href="/wiki/The_West_Australian" title="The West Australian">The West Australian</a> Good Food Guide 2011</i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-miettas_review_8-1"><a href="#cite_note-miettas_review-8">[8]</a></sup> and retained its one star rating for 2012<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-miettas_review_8-2"><a href="#cite_note-miettas_review-8">[8]</a></sup> and 2013,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-broadfield2013_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-broadfield2013-9">[9]</a></sup> but lost that rating for 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-broadfield2014_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-broadfield2014-10">[10]</a></sup>
</p><p>Greenhouse also featured in an episode of <a href="/wiki/MasterChef_Australia_(series_5)" title="MasterChef Australia (series 5)"><i>MasterChef Australia</i> series 5</a> in 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-perthnow_17-04-2013_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-perthnow_17-04-2013-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-smh_04-07-2013_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-smh_04-07-2013-12">[12]</a></sup>
</p><p>The restaurant closed in May 2017. Managing partners of the venue claimed decreased revenue and high operation costs as contributing factors<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup>.
</p>
