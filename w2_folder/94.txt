<p><b>Jordan Green Building Council</b> (<a href="/wiki/Arabic" title="Arabic">Arabic</a>: المجلس الأردني للأبنية الخضراء) is a <a href="/wiki/Jordan" title="Jordan">Jordanian</a> member-based organization established in 2009. It received its “Established Member” status after the formal acceptance from <a href="/wiki/World_Green_Building_Council" title="World Green Building Council">World Green Building Council</a> in April 2012. Its mission is to promote and advocate for the adoption of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Green_Building" title="Green Building">Green Building</a> Practices in all phases of the building process leading towards making Green Buildings a widespread reality in <a href="/wiki/Jordan" title="Jordan">Jordan</a>. The Council is part of a global network of more than 80 GBCs worldwide and holds the authority to represent the World Green Building Council (WGBC) in Jordan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
