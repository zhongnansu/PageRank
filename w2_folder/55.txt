<p><i><b>Greenhouse</b></i> is the eighth studio album of the American jazz group <a href="/wiki/Yellowjackets" title="Yellowjackets">Yellowjackets</a>, released in 1991. In this album, the group was a trio. <a href="/wiki/Bob_Mintzer" title="Bob Mintzer">Bob Mintzer</a>, who became an official member from next album, was credited as a guest artist. The album reached a peak position of number one on <i><a href="/wiki/Billboard_(magazine)" title="Billboard (magazine)">Billboard</a></i><span class="nowrap" style="padding-left:0.1em;">'s</span> Top Contemporary Jazz Albums chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Charts_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Charts-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
