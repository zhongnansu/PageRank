<p><b>Green Business Certification Inc.</b> (<b>GBCI</b>) is an American organization that provides third-party credentialing and verification for several rating systems relating to the built environment.  It was established as the <b>Green Building Certification Institute</b> in January 2008 with the support of the <a href="/wiki/U.S._Green_Building_Council" title="U.S. Green Building Council">U.S. Green Building Council</a>  to provide independent oversight of the <a href="/wiki/Leadership_in_Energy_and_Environmental_Design" title="Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design">Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design</a> (LEED) project certification and professional credentialing processes.  The current name was adopted on 16 April 2015 after the organization began providing the same service for the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=International_WELL_Building_Institute&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="International WELL Building Institute (page does not exist)">International WELL Building Institute</a>'s <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=WELL_Building_Standard&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="WELL Building Standard (page does not exist)">WELL Building Standard</a>, the Perfect Power Institute's PEER standard, and the Global Real Estate Sustainability Benchmark.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="LEED_Professional_Credentials">LEED Professional Credentials</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Green_Business_Certification_Inc.&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: LEED Professional Credentials">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The suite of LEED professional credentials was designed to denote leadership in green building, with the LEED Accredited Professional (AP) with specialty credential distinguishing building professionals with the knowledge and skills to successfully steward the LEED certification process. Credentialing exams and designations administered by GBCI include the LEED Green Associate, LEED AP with specialty and LEED Fellow, as well as the LEED for Homes Green Rater and Green Classroom Professional certificates. The LEED professional credential exams are ANSI 17024 accredited.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="LEED_Project_Certification">LEED Project Certification</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Green_Business_Certification_Inc.&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: LEED Project Certification">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>GBCI administers the LEED certification program, performing third-party technical reviews and verification of LEED-registered projects to determine if they have met the standards set forth by the LEED rating system. As of April 2013, the total footprint of LEED-certified projects is nearly 3 billion square feet, and nearly eight billion additional square feet are registered with GBCI for LEED certification.
</p>
