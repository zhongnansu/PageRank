<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">GreenXC</h1> <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div> <div id="contentSub"></div>
<div id="jump-to-nav"></div> <a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>GreenXC</b>, established in 2011,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is an organization that works to raise awareness for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/National_Parks" title="National Parks">National Parks</a>, National Forests and the <a href="/wiki/National_Park_Foundation" title="National Park Foundation">National Park Foundation</a>, the official charity of America’s nearly 400 national parks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> It focuses on the youth and reaches out to connect them to the parks and forests in order to ensure the parks are supported for the next generation of taxpayers. GreenXC aligns itself with the National Park Foundation's goal of being "deeply committed to engaging the country's youth into a lifelong experience with the parks."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>GreenXC also works closely with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/USDA" title="USDA">USDA</a> <a href="/wiki/United_States_Forest_Service" title="United States Forest Service">United States Forest Service</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-USDA_Blog_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-USDA_Blog-4">[4]</a></sup> on setting destinations for their cross-country awareness campaign during the summer of 2011. They will be visiting and touring various sites and conducting interviews which they will share with their readers through their site. The purpose of the campaign is to encourage the young generation to have a more active involvement with their environment.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Project_Summer_2011_-_Rideshare_Cross_Country">Project Summer 2011 - Rideshare Cross Country</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=GreenXC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Project Summer 2011 - Rideshare Cross Country">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2011 GreenXC is set to travel cross country to raise awareness for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/National_Parks" title="National Parks">National Parks</a> and <a href="/wiki/United_States_National_Forest" title="United States National Forest">National Forests</a>. "GreenXC, an ensemble of green-minded young professionals, has a mission to travel cross-country solely by ride-shares. A unique twist to a traditional road trip, <a href="/wiki/Peer-to-peer_ridesharing" title="Peer-to-peer ridesharing">rideshares</a> make for great adventures as eco-enthusiastic travelers connect, journey together and help reduce <a href="/wiki/Carbon_footprint" title="Carbon footprint">carbon footprints</a>. Roadtrippers find one another via <a href="/wiki/Blog" title="Blog">blogs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Social_media" title="Social media">social media</a>, even <a href="/wiki/Craigslist" title="Craigslist">Craig’s List</a>."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-saving_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-saving-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p><p>"GreenXC will collect and document the stories of the people who help turn these ambitions into a reality. The experiences will be shared through daily blog posts and video footage on <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTube</a>."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-saving_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-saving-5">[5]</a></sup> Those following can interact and donate directly to the parks visited. The route of the trip will be <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crowd_source" title="Crowd source">crowd sourced</a> through suggestions on <a href="/wiki/Twitter" title="Twitter">Twitter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Facebook" title="Facebook">Facebook</a>, the GreenXC blog/forum and social sites like <a href="/wiki/Reddit" title="Reddit">Reddit</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Focus_on_Young_Generation">Focus on Young Generation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=GreenXC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Focus on Young Generation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>GreenXC aligns itself with the Federal Government's goal of promoting the National Parks and Forests to the young generation.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> In 2011, the President set as one of the top goals of the Federal Government to promote the great outdoors and especially to the youth. "Special attention should be given to bringing young Americans into the conversation."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>
