<p>The <b>Greene-Lewis House</b> is a historic home in <a href="/wiki/Tallahassee,_Florida" title="Tallahassee, Florida">Tallahassee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Florida" title="Florida">Florida</a>. It is located at 535 West College Avenue. On June 11, 1998, it was added to the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">U.S.</a> <a href="/wiki/National_Register_of_Historic_Places" title="National Register of Historic Places">National Register of Historic Places</a>.
</p>
