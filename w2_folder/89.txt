<p><b>Global Green USA</b> is the U.S. arm of <a href="/wiki/Green_Cross_International" title="Green Cross International">Green Cross International</a>, founded by <a href="/wiki/Mikhail_Gorbachev" title="Mikhail Gorbachev">President Gorbachev</a> to "foster a global shift toward a sustainable and secure future".  It is one of 30 national offices with over 70 professional staff worldwide.  Global Green USA is a national <a href="/wiki/Environmental_organization" title="Environmental organization">environmental organization</a>.
</p><p>Global Green USA has raised more than twenty billion dollars' worth of building construction<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Please_clarify" title="Wikipedia:Please clarify"><span title="The text near this tag may need clarification or removal of jargon. (June 2017)">clarification needed</span></a></i>]</sup> by encouraging the integration of green building and sustainability practices. The organization raises money through annual events, with the premier being their Pre-Oscar Gala.
</p><p>Programs include City Partnerships, University Education Partnerships, Pilot and Outreach, and Affordable Housing. 
</p><p>The U.S. headquarters is in <a href="/wiki/Santa_Monica,_California" title="Santa Monica, California">Santa Monica, California</a>, and that office is responsible for LEED neighborhood development, bike sharing, Los Angeles City Policy, and eco districting. 
</p>
