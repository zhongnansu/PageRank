<p><b>Evergreen</b> is a former <a href="/wiki/Census-designated_place" title="Census-designated place">census-designated place</a> (CDP) in <a href="/wiki/Marathon_County,_Wisconsin" title="Marathon County, Wisconsin">Marathon County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wisconsin" title="Wisconsin">Wisconsin</a>, United States. The population was 3,611 at the <a href="/wiki/2000_United_States_Census" title="2000 United States Census">2000 census</a>. Evergreen is now part of the village of <a href="/wiki/Kronenwetter,_Wisconsin" title="Kronenwetter, Wisconsin">Kronenwetter</a>, which was incorporated on June 17, 2003.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Marathon_County,_Wisconsin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Evergreen is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Evergreen,_Marathon_County,_Wisconsin&amp;params=44_50_50_N_89_37_39_W_region:US_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">44°50′50″N</span> <span class="longitude">89°37′39″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">44.84722°N 89.62750°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">44.84722; -89.62750</span></span></span></a></span> (44.847312, -89.627491).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR1_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR1-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the CDP had a total area of 3.8 square miles (9.9 km²), all of it land.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Marathon_County,_Wisconsin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR2-3">[3]</a></sup> of 2000, there were 3,611 people, 1,228 households, and 1,015 families residing in the CDP. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 946.5 people per square mile (365.9/km²). There were 1,267 housing units at an average density of 332.1/sq mi (128.4/km²). The racial makeup of the CDP was 98.20% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 0.08% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, 0.22% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 1.11% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_(U.S._Census)" title="Asian (U.S. Census)">Asian</a>, 0.17% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(United_States_Census)" title="Race (United States Census)">other races</a>, and 0.22% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 0.22% of the population.
</p><p>There were 1,228 households out of which 46.3% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 72.9% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 6.5% had a female householder with no husband present, and 17.3% were non-families. 11.8% of all households were made up of individuals and 2.3% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.91 and the average family size was 3.18.
</p><p>In the CDP, the population was spread out with 30.6% under the age of 18, 7.2% from 18 to 24, 32.5% from 25 to 44, 25.0% from 45 to 64, and 4.7% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 34 years. For every 100 females, there were 104.4 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 102.8 males.
</p><p>The median income for a household in the CDP was $58,774, and the median income for a family was $61,699. Males had a median income of $38,784 versus $27,796 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the CDP was $21,467. About 4.6% of families and 4.6% of the population were below the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">poverty line</a>, including 5.9% of those under age 18 and 12.9% of those age 65 or over.
</p>
