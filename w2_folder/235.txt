<p><b>Evergreen</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> and <a href="/wiki/Census-designated_place" title="Census-designated place">census-designated place</a> (CDP) in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Tatums_Township,_Columbus_County,_North_Carolina&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Tatums Township, Columbus County, North Carolina (page does not exist)">Tatums Township</a>, <a href="/wiki/Columbus_County,_North_Carolina" title="Columbus County, North Carolina">Columbus County</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina" title="North Carolina">North Carolina</a>, United States. It lies on <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina_Highway_242" title="North Carolina Highway 242">North Carolina Highway 242</a> north of <a href="/wiki/U.S._Route_74" title="U.S. Route 74">U.S. Route 74</a> and <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina_Highway_130" title="North Carolina Highway 130">NC 130</a>, at an elevation of 108 feet (33 m). The population was 420 at the 2010 census.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census_2010_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census_2010-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Tatums_Township,_Columbus_County,_North_Carolina&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Evergreen is located near the northwestern edge of Columbus County and is bordered to the northwest by the town of <a href="/wiki/Boardman,_North_Carolina" title="Boardman, North Carolina">Boardman</a>. U.S. Route 74, a four-lane highway, forms the southern edge of the CDP and leads northwest 19 miles (31 km) to <a href="/wiki/Interstate_95" title="Interstate 95">Interstate 95</a> near <a href="/wiki/Lumberton,_North_Carolina" title="Lumberton, North Carolina">Lumberton</a> and southeast 14 miles (23 km) to <a href="/wiki/Whiteville,_North_Carolina" title="Whiteville, North Carolina">Whiteville</a>, the Columbus County seat.
</p><p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the Evergreen CDP has a total area of 3.9 square miles (10.0 km<sup>2</sup>), all of it land.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census_2010_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Census_2010-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
