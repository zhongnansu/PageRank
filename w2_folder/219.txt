<p><b>Evergreen</b>, or <b>Evergreen Community</b>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Parkbridge_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Parkbridge-6">[6]</a></sup> is a neighbourhood in the rural northeast portion of the City of <a href="/wiki/Edmonton" title="Edmonton">Edmonton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alberta" title="Alberta">Alberta</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>. A <a href="/wiki/Manufactured_housing" title="Manufactured housing">manufactured home</a> community, it is located at the northwest corner of 167 Avenue NW and Meridian Street.
</p><p>Evergreen had a population of 1,450 according to Edmonton's 2012 municipal census.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Evergreen_2012_Census_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Evergreen_2012_Census-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p><p>The community is represented by the Edmonton Evergreen <a href="/wiki/Neighborhood_council" title="Neighborhood council">Community Association</a>, established in 1982, which runs a <a href="/wiki/Community_centre" title="Community centre">community hall</a> located at Evergreen Drive and Cedar Avenue.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Edmonton&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the City of Edmonton's 2016 municipal census, Evergreen had a population of <span class="sortkey" style="display:none">7003150400000000000♠</span>1,504 living in <span class="sortkey" style="display:none">7002686000000000000♠</span>686 dwellings,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2016population_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-2016population-5">[5]</a></sup> a -2.4% change from its 2014 population of <span class="sortkey" style="display:none">7003150100000000000♠</span>1,501.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2014population_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-2014population-10">[10]</a></sup> With a land area of 0.66 km<sup>2</sup> (0.25 sq mi),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nbhds_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-nbhds-4">[4]</a></sup> it had a population density of <span class="sortkey" style="display:none">7003227880000000000♠</span>2,278.8 people/km<sup>2</sup> in 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2016population_5-2"><a href="#cite_note-2016population-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nbhds_4-2"><a href="#cite_note-nbhds-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Black_Friday">Black Friday</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Edmonton&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Black Friday">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Edmonton_Tornado" title="Edmonton Tornado">Edmonton Tornado</a></div>
<p>In 1987, fifteen people in Evergreen were killed and almost 200 homes were destroyed or damaged beyond repair when the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Edmonton_Tornado" title="Edmonton Tornado">Edmonton Tornado</a> swept through the community at the north end of its 40 km (25 mi) path of death and destruction.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Evergreen_Edmonton_Tornado_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-Evergreen_Edmonton_Tornado-11">[11]</a></sup>
</p>
