<p><i><b>Greenhouse Academy</b></i> is a tween drama television series released by <a href="/wiki/Netflix" title="Netflix">Netflix</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Variety_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Variety-1">[1]</a></sup> Based on the Israeli television series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Greenhouse_(TV_series)" title="The Greenhouse (TV series)">The Greenhouse</a></i> (<i>Ha-Hamama</i>), created by Giora Chamizer, the series was adapted for international audiences by Chamizer and Paula Yoo.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Deadline_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Deadline-2">[2]</a></sup> The first season of the series was released on Netflix on September 8, 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-S1-release_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-S1-release-3">[3]</a></sup> Netflix renewed the series for a third season in March 2018.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Greenhouse_Academy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eight months after losing their astronaut mother in a rocket explosion, brother and a sister, Alex and Hayley Woods, enroll at a private boarding school for gifted future leaders. Separately, they join two competing houses within the school and become rivals. Soon, mysterious events draw the students from both houses into a secret investigation. They uncover a deadly plot to use earthquakes for monetary gain. Only by joining forces and working together will they be able to stop this scheme.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Production">Production</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Greenhouse_Academy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Production">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Greenhouse Academy</i> is a Netflix original series based on the Israeli tween-drama <i><a href="/wiki/The_Greenhouse_(TV_series)" title="The Greenhouse (TV series)">The Greenhouse</a></i> (<i>Ha-Hamama</i>), which ran on <a href="/wiki/Nickelodeon_(Israel)" title="Nickelodeon (Israel)">Nickelodeon Israel</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kidscreen1_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Kidscreen1-7">[7]</a></sup> Both versions were created by Giora Chamizer and produced by Nutz Productions, a subsidiary of Ananey Communications.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ananey_Communications_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Ananey_Communications-8">[8]</a></sup> Two seasons consisting of a total of 24 episodes were produced. The series was filmed in summer 2016 in Tel Aviv and at other locations in Israel.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TimesofIsrael_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-TimesofIsrael-9">[9]</a></sup> The first season was released on Netflix on September 8, 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-S1-release_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-S1-release-3">[3]</a></sup> On January 18, 2018, the official Greenhouse Academy Instagram account announced that season 2 would be released on Netflix on February 14, 2018.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> The series was "quietly" renewed for a third season in March 2018.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RenewCancel_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-RenewCancel-11">[11]</a></sup> In September 2018, the role of Brooke Osmond was recast with <a href="/wiki/Danika_Yarosh" title="Danika Yarosh">Danika Yarosh</a>, after Grace Van Dien was cast in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Village_(2019_TV_series)" title="The Village (2019 TV series)">The Village</a></i> which was picked up to series by <a href="/wiki/NBC" title="NBC">NBC</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2018%E2%80%9319_United_States_network_television_schedule" title="2018â€“19 United States network television schedule">2018â€“19 U.S. television season</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-recasting_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-recasting-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
