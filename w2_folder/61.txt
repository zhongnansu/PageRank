<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Greenhouse (car)</h1> <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div> <div id="contentSub"></div>
<div id="jump-to-nav"></div> <a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:382px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Hofmeisterknick-e36.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="189" data-file-width="380" height="189" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/99/Hofmeisterknick-e36.jpg" width="380"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Hofmeisterknick-e36.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Hofmeister_kink" title="Hofmeister kink">Hofmeister kink</a> on a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/BMW_E36" title="BMW E36">BMW E36 325is</a></div></div></div>
<p>The <b>greenhouse</b> (or <b>glasshouse</b>) of a <a href="/wiki/Car" title="Car">car</a> comprises the <a href="/wiki/Windshield" title="Windshield">windshield</a>, rear and side windows, the <a href="/wiki/Pillar_(car)" title="Pillar (car)">pillars</a> separating them (designated A-pillar, B-pillar and so on, starting from the car's front), and the car's roof.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>The shape and position of the greenhouse have a defining influence on the looks of the car and are a prime factor in differentiating between body styles such as saloon/<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sedan_(car)" title="Sedan (car)">sedan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Coup%C3%A9" title="Coupé">coupé</a>, <a href="/wiki/Station_wagon" title="Station wagon">estate/wagon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hatchback" title="Hatchback">hatchback</a>.
</p><p>In the 2000s and 2010s, greenhouses have become narrower because of design trends and crash regulations.
</p><p>Some manufacturers incorporate trademark design features in the greenhouse that are present across several model series, one example being the <a href="/wiki/BMW" title="BMW">BMW</a> <a href="/wiki/Hofmeister_kink" title="Hofmeister kink">Hofmeister kink</a>.
</p>
