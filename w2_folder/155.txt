<p>The <b>Peter Greene House</b> is a historic house at 1124 West Shore Road in <a href="/wiki/Warwick,_Rhode_Island" title="Warwick, Rhode Island">Warwick, Rhode Island</a>.  The 2-1/2 story wood frame house was built c. 1751, and is a rare surviving 18th-century house in Warwick.  It has a five-bay facade with a plain door surround, a central chimney, and a rear ell.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NRHP_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-NRHP-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>The house was listed on the <a href="/wiki/National_Register_of_Historic_Places" title="National Register of Historic Places">National Register of Historic Places</a> in 1983.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nris_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-nris-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
