<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">MillionMile Greenway</h1> <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div> <div id="contentSub"></div>
<div id="jump-to-nav"></div> <a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>MillionMile Greenway</b> (MMG) is an organization and system of connected <a href="/wiki/Greenway_(landscape)" title="Greenway (landscape)">greenways</a> across metropolitan <a href="/wiki/Atlanta" title="Atlanta">Atlanta</a>, the state of <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(U.S._state)" title="Georgia (U.S. state)">Georgia</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Eastern_United_States" title="Eastern United States">eastern United States</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> MMG intends to help individuals and local communities begin or expand their efforts at <a href="/wiki/Conservation_(ethic)" title="Conservation (ethic)">conservation</a> and <a href="/wiki/Recreation" title="Recreation">recreation</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Purpose">Purpose</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=MillionMile_Greenway&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Purpose">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The MillionMile Greenway<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> is a non-profit organization which acts as a mentor for people who want to preserve <a href="/wiki/Open_space_reserve" title="Open space reserve">greenspace</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Each section of the MillionMile Greenway aims to conserve greenspace, provide recreation for the public and connect to an existing greenspace. The MillionMile Greenway aims to achieve a balance between <a href="/wiki/Population_growth" title="Population growth">population growth</a> and conservation of land and <a href="/wiki/Water_resources" title="Water resources">water resources</a> for parks, trails and natural spaces.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
