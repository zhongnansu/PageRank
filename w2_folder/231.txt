<p><b>Evergreen Township</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Civil_township" title="Civil township">civil township</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sanilac_County,_Michigan" title="Sanilac County, Michigan">Sanilac County</a> in the <a href="/wiki/U.S._state" title="U.S. state">U.S. state</a> of <a href="/wiki/Michigan" title="Michigan">Michigan</a>. The population was 995 at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_States_Census,_2000" title="United States Census, 2000">2000 census</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen_Township,_Sanilac_County,_Michigan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the township has a total area of 35.4 square miles (92 km<sup>2</sup>), all land.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen_Township,_Sanilac_County,_Michigan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-GR2-1">[1]</a></sup> of 2000, there were 995 people, 345 households, and 265 families residing in the township.  The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 28.1 per square mile (10.9/km²).  There were 380 housing units at an average density of 10.7 per square mile (4.1/km²).  The racial makeup of the township was 96.08% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 0.80% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, 0.30% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 1.11% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(United_States_Census)" title="Race (United States Census)">other races</a>, and 1.71% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 1.61% of the population.
</p><p>There were 345 households out of which 33.0% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 62.0% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 9.0% had a female householder with no husband present, and 22.9% were non-families. 16.5% of all households were made up of individuals and 5.8% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older.  The average household size was 2.87 and the average family size was 3.19.
</p><p>In the township the population was spread out with 27.4% under the age of 18, 10.4% from 18 to 24, 27.6% from 25 to 44, 23.4% from 45 to 64, and 11.2% who were 65 years of age or older.  The median age was 36 years. For every 100 females, there were 118.2 males.  For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 111.1 males.
</p><p>The median income for a household in the township was $36,202, and the median income for a family was $38,250. Males had a median income of $28,077 versus $22,941 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the township was $15,814.  About 4.9% of families and 8.2% of the population were below the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">poverty line</a>, including 5.1% of those under age 18 and 5.7% of those age 65 or over.
</p>
