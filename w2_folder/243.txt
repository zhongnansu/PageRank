<p><b>Evergreen</b> is a town in <a href="/wiki/Langlade_County,_Wisconsin" title="Langlade County, Wisconsin">Langlade County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wisconsin" title="Wisconsin">Wisconsin</a>, United States. The population was 468 as of the 2000 census. The unincorporated communities of <a href="/wiki/Four_Corners,_Langlade_County,_Wisconsin" title="Four Corners, Langlade County, Wisconsin">Four Corners</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Elton,_Wisconsin" title="Elton, Wisconsin">Elton</a> is located in the town.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Langlade_County,_Wisconsin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the town has a total area of 36.2 square miles (93.9 km²), of which, 35.8 square miles (92.8 km²) of it is land and 0.4 square miles (1.1 km²) of it (1.13%) is water.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Evergreen,_Langlade_County,_Wisconsin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-GR2-2">[2]</a></sup> of 2000, there were 468 people, 181 households, and 135 families residing in the town.  The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 13.1 people per square mile (5.0/km²).  There were 218 housing units at an average density of 6.1 per square mile (2.3/km²).  The racial makeup of the town was 97.65% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 0.43% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 0.85% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_(U.S._Census)" title="Asian (U.S. Census)">Asian</a>, and 1.07% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 0.43% of the population.
</p><p>There were 181 households out of which 29.3% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 64.1% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 5.5% had a female householder with no husband present, and 25.4% were non-families. 23.2% of all households were made up of individuals and 13.3% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older.  The average household size was 2.59 and the average family size was 3.05.
</p><p>In the town, the population was spread out with 27.4% under the age of 18, 4.1% from 18 to 24, 23.7% from 25 to 44, 28.4% from 45 to 64, and 16.5% who were 65 years of age or older.  The median age was 42 years. For every 100 females, there were 113.7 males.  For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 111.2 males.
</p><p>The median income for a household in the town was $30,536, and the median income for a family was $36,250. Males had a median income of $29,205 versus $18,125 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the town was $16,519.  About 5.3% of families and 6.0% of the population were below the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">poverty line</a>, including 4.1% of those under age 18 and 16.9% of those age 65 or over.
</p>
