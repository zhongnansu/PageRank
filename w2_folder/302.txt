<p>The <b>Oxygen Green Party</b> (<a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <i lang="es">Partido Verde Oxígeno</i>) was a <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">political party</a> in <a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">Colombia</a> founded in 1998. After <a href="/wiki/%C3%8Dngrid_Betancourt" title="Íngrid Betancourt">Íngrid Betancourt</a>, one of its most prominent members, was kidnapped in 2002, the party's popular support began to fade. In 2005, a political reform on the Colombian party system left the party without participation, due to low popular support.
</p>
